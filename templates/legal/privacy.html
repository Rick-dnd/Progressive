Privacy Policy

Information about the protection of your personal data

1. Privacy at a Glance

The protection of your personal data is important to us. This privacy policy informs you about how we handle your data and what rights you have.

The following notes provide a simple overview of what happens to your personal data when you visit this website. Personal data is any data that can personally identify you.

We do not collect data from users of our website. No cookies are set on our website and no tracking takes place. We pay special attention to the protection of your privacy and only collect the data that you actively provide to us through the contact form.

We do not use cookies and do not conduct user analysis.

2. Contact Form

When you send us inquiries via the contact form, your details from the inquiry form, including the contact data you provided there, will be stored by us for the purpose of processing the inquiry and in the event of follow-up questions. We do not share this data without your consent.

Your data is transmitted encrypted and stored securely.
